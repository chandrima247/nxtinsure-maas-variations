About J-Care Junior

J-Care Junior is Jubilee Health's flagship child health plan, designed to give parents complete peace of mind. From routine outpatient visits to major hospitalisation, J-Care covers your child at every step — with fast digital claims and access to Uganda's widest hospital network.

How to make a claim
1

Notify us within 48 hours

Call our 24/7 helpline on 0800 100 200 or submit a claim notification in the NxtInsure app. For emergencies, go directly to any network hospital — cashless treatment available.

⚡ Instant pre-auth for emergencies
2

Submit your documents

Upload your medical reports, receipts, and discharge summary. Our team reviews documents within 4 working hours. You can track status in real time.

Our Uganda-based claims team reviews your case. For claims under UGX 500,000, auto-approval is triggered within 2 hours. Complex cases reviewed within 48 hours.
